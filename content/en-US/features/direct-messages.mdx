# Direct Messages

Alkemio includes a built-in messaging feature that lets you have private conversations with other members, without leaving the platform.

## How to Open the Chat

Click the **chat button** in the bottom-right corner of any page. A panel opens showing all your conversations. From here you can read and send messages, search for a conversation, or start a new one.

The chat button shows a badge with the number of conversations that have unread messages.

## Starting a Conversation

To start a new direct message:
1. Click the chat button to open the panel.
2. Click **New message**.
3. Search for and select the person you want to message.
4. Your conversation opens. Start typing.

## Reactions

You can react to any message with an emoji. Reactions are visible to everyone in the conversation and update in real time.

## Tips

- Use direct messages for quick questions, feedback, or follow-ups with specific members.
- The chat panel works on mobile too. Tap the chat button to open it.
