# Delete your account

It is not possible to delete your Alkemio account yourself. This is something the Alkemio team handles for you. Simply reach out and we'll take care of it.

## How to request account deletion

Contact our support team via one of the following options:

- 📧 Email **[support@alkem.io](mailto:support@alkem.io)**
- Or fill in the **[Contact Form](https://welcome.alkem.io/contact/)** on our website

Please include your full name and the email address linked to your account so we can process your request quickly.

## Please note

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Any Spaces you host will also be affected — if you want to transfer them to another admin first, let us know and we'll help you arrange that.
