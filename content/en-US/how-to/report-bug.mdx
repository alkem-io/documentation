# Raise a bug / flag an issue

Spotted something that isn't working as expected? Here's how to get in touch with the Alkemio support team.

## Option 1: From within the platform

1. Click on your **profile picture** in the top right corner.
2. Click **Get Help**.
3. Click **Contact the Team** to open the support page and reach out directly.

## Option 2: Via the Support page

Scroll to the bottom of the platform and click **Support**. From there you can:

- Send an email to **support@alkem.io**
- Or scroll down and fill in the **[Contact Form](https://welcome.alkem.io/contact/)** with your name, email, and a description of the issue

## What to include in your report

To help the team resolve your issue as quickly as possible, include:

- A short description of what happened
- The steps you took before the issue occurred
- The name of the Space or Subspace where the issue occurred (if relevant)
- A screenshot if possible
