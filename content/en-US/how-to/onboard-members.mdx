# Onboard your first members & manage join requests

You've set up your Space — now it's time to bring in your community. This guide walks you through inviting people and handling incoming join requests.

## Invite people to your Space

As a Space admin, go to **Space Settings > Community tab**. On the right side of the screen you have two ways to invite people:

- **People already on Alkemio** — search by name, select the correct user, and send the invite
- **People not yet on Alkemio** — type their email address to send them an invitation

## Manage join requests

If your Space requires approval for new members, incoming requests will appear in the **Community** tab under **Pending applications & invitations**.

To review and approve a request:
1. Navigate to **Space Settings**.
2. Click the **Community** tab.
3. Go to **Pending applications & invitations**.
4. Click the **eye icon** (👁) next to a person to view their application form.
5. Click the **checkmark** to approve or the **cross** to reject.

Once approved, the user becomes a member of your Space with the **User** role.

> **Note:** You can only manage join requests if you are a Space admin.

## Tips for a smooth onboarding

- Before inviting your full community, engage your **champions** first — people who are familiar with the platform and can help others get started.
- Consider sending an **Update** to introduce the Space and set expectations.
- Add an introductory Post on the **Home** tab so new members immediately understand the purpose and goals of the Space.
