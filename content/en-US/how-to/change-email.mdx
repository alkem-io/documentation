# Change your email address

You cannot change your email address yourself, but our support team can update it for you. Simply reach out and we'll take care of it.

## How to request an email address change

Get in touch with our support team via one of the following options:

- Click on your profile picture in the top right corner, click **Get Help**, then click **Contact the Team**
- 📧 Email **support@alkem.io**
- Or fill in the **[Contact Form](https://welcome.alkem.io/contact/)** on our website

Please include your full name, your current email address, and the new email address you'd like to use. We'll get back to you within 2 business days.
