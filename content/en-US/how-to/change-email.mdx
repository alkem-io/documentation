# Change your email address

It is currently not possible to change the email address linked to your Alkemio account. However, we're happy to help you with the transition.

## What to do instead

The recommended approach is to **create a new account** using the email address you want to use going forward.

You can sign up for a new account at [alkem.io](https://alkem.io).

## Need help with your old account?

We understand this may raise a few questions — for example, what to do with your old account, how to transfer your Spaces, or how to be added to existing Spaces with your new account. Our support team is happy to help with any of these.

Get in touch via:
- 📧 **[support@alkem.io](mailto:support@alkem.io)**
- Or use the **[Contact Form](https://welcome.alkem.io/contact/)** on our website

We'll get back to you within 2 business days.
