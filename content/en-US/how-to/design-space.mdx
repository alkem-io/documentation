# Design your Space

A well-designed Space makes it easier for members to understand what it's about, navigate confidently, and feel engaged. This guide helps you make your Space visually attractive and clearly structured.

## Make it look inviting

Go to the **About** sub-section of your Space settings. Here you can:

- Add a compelling **tagline** that communicates your Space's purpose at a glance
- Upload **visuals** (banner image and logo) — these appear on the Alkemio home page and your Space's dashboard
- Write a **What**, **Why**, and **Who** description to clearly frame your Space
- Add **tags** to make your Space easier to find
- Include **references** such as your website, relevant articles, or an introduction video

> **Tip:** Use bold, headings, and images in the description fields to make the text more readable and engaging.

## Rename and organise your tabs

Go to the **Layout** sub-section to rename the default tabs — Home, Community, Subspaces, and Knowledge — to language that fits your community. For example: Overview, People, Projects, and Background.

You can also **drag and drop Posts** between tabs to put the right content in the right place.

## Set up your community roles

Under the **Community** sub-section, you can assign the right roles to the right people:

- **Members** — contribute insights and experiences
- **Leads** — visible as drivers of the Space's activity
- **Admins** — manage content in the backend without being visible as leads
- **Host** — responsible for moderating content (managed by the Alkemio team)

## Control what members can do

In the **Settings** sub-section, define what your members are allowed to do — for example, whether they can create Subspaces or only admins can.

## Add your first content

Consider adding an introductory Post on the Home tab to welcome members and explain what the Space is for. A collection of links on the Knowledge tab is a great way to share background reading and resources.
