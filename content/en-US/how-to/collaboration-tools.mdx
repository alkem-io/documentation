# Use Alkemio's collaboration tools

Alkemio offers a range of built-in tools to help your community share knowledge, discuss ideas, and work together — all within your Space or Subspace.

## Posts

Posts are the core building block for collaboration on Alkemio. Use them to share information, start discussions, or gather input from your community. Every Post has a title and optional description, and you can add:

- **Additional content:**
  - **Whiteboard** — for real-time visual collaboration (brainstorming, mapping, planning)
  - **Collection of Links and Documents** — curate and share resources from around the web or from uploads
  - **Call for Whiteboards** — invite members to contribute their own visual responses
  - **Call for Posts** — ask members to submit written responses
  - **Call to Action** — a clickable button that sends members to a specific URL or action

- **Response Options:**
  - **Comments** — allow members to reply to the Post
  - **Reactions** — members can react with emojis to show appreciation or agreement
  - **Collection of Whiteboards** — ask members to respond with their own visual whiteboard

> **Where can you add Posts?** Depending on settings, admins or all members can add Posts on any tab — Home, Community, Subspaces, or Knowledge.

## iFrame

Want to embed a video, a form, or an external tool directly into a Post? Use an **iFrame**. You can embed content from whitelisted sites by pasting the embed code into the iFrame field of a Post.

> **Note:** Only whitelisted sites can be embedded. If your source is not supported, email support@alkem.io to request it to be added.

## Video Call (Jitsi)

Alkemio has a built-in video conferencing tool powered by **Jitsi**. To start a call:

1. Go to your Space or Subspace.
2. Click the **video icon** (top right in a Space, left side in a Subspace).
3. The first person to open the call becomes the host.
4. Other participants can then join.

As host, you can secure the call by setting a **lobby** or a **password** via the three-dot menu (⋯) at the bottom right during the call.

## Virtual Contributors (VCs)

Virtual Contributors are AI-powered teammates you can add to your Space. A VC is trained on specific knowledge — from your Subspace content, uploaded documents, or your own AI engine — so it gives focused, relevant responses.

To interact with a VC, **tag it in a Post** using its name (e.g. @GreenIdeasVC). It will reply directly in the thread.

> **Note:** Not all users have permission to create VCs. Contact the Alkemio team if you'd like access.

## Events

Admins can add events to any Space or Subspace to keep members informed about upcoming meetings, milestones, or sessions. To add an event:

1. Click the **+** button in the Events block.
2. Fill in the title, event type, date, time, description, location, and tags.
3. Click **Save** — the event is immediately visible to all members.

Members can view events in a **List View** or **Calendar View**.

## Updates

Use Updates to broadcast important messages to all members of your Space. Members receive an email notification when an update is posted (if notifications are enabled). To post an update:

1. Go to **Settings > Updates from the Leads** in your Space.
2. Type your message in the text box.
3. Format it if needed, then click **Post Update**.
