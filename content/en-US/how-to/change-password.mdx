# Change your password

You can change your password directly from your account settings. If you're logged out and have forgotten your password, you can also reset it via the login page.

## Option 1: Change your password while logged in

1. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Security**.
3. Under **Change password**, enter your new password and confirm it.
4. Click **Save**.

## Option 2: Reset your password via the login page

If you're logged out and don't remember your password:

1. Go to the login page and click **Sign in**.
2. Click the **Forgot password?** link below the password field.
3. Enter your email address and click **Continue**.
4. You will receive a recovery link by email. Click it to set a new password.

## Don't see the email?

Check your spam or junk folder. The recovery email is sometimes filtered by email clients.
