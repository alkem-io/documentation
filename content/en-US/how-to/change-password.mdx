# Change your password

Forgot your password or want to set a new one? You can reset it in a few steps from the Alkemio login page.

## Steps to reset your password

1. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner of the platform.
2. Click **Sign out** to log out of your account.
3. On the login page, click **Sign in**.
4. Click **Forgot password?** below the password field.
5. Enter your email address and click **Continue**.
6. You will receive a recovery link by email — click it to set a new password.

## Don't see the email?

Check your spam or junk folder. The recovery email is sometimes filtered by email clients.
