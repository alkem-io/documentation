## Accessibility 

### We strive to support WCAG level A  

In 2023, we conducted an initial evaluation in collaboration with our partners and implemented our first improvements
You can learn more about this in [our blog post](https://welcome.alkem.io/blog/2024-03-accessibility/).
We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing effort and welcome collaboration to continue enhancing our platform. 

### Designed for Desktop, Friendly on Mobile 

Alkemio is best used on desktop, but we do ensure that the platform is mobile friendly.
