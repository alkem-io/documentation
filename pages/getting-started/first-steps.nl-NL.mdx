# Get Started

1. 📷 **Create and fill out your Alkemio profile [2 min]**\
   Don’t forget to add a nice picture of yourself :D. We want everyone to know who they are cooperating with to build a relationship of trust within this community. Filling out your profile, including your skills and interests, will allow others to find you when they can use your expertise! [Use this link to create an account if you haven’t already.](https://identity.alkem.io/login?returnUrl=https%3a%2f%2falkem.io/home)

2. 📁 **Improve your understanding of the platform structure [5 min]**\
   The Alkemio platform has been designed carefully to support its users in making progress in Spaces and Subspaces. This requires some structure and terminology that you might not be familiar with. You can read more about these in the Structure section of this document, in the navigation menu on the left side of the page.

3. 🔎 **Join a Space and explore it [5 min]**\
   There are many Spaces on Alkemio, most of which are open to anyone. For example, take a look at the [Welcome Space](https://alkem.io/welcome-space/dashboard).

4. 👋 **Introduce yourself in the Welcome Space [1 min]**\
   Meet your fellow Alkemio Community members and invite them to your Space or Subspace community.
