# Navigation
Go to your account page of on your profile by
- Click on your profile picture on the top right corner of the screen
- Click on MY Profile
- Click on the gear icon right of your name
- Go to the ACCOUNT tab
- Click the + icon in the Hosted Spaces box
- Fill out the details of the Space
