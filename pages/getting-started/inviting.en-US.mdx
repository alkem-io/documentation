# Navigation
To invite people to your space, go to the settings of your space by clicking the gear icon on the right.
![Gear Icon](/space-settings-gear-icon.png)
Next, go to the community page of the settings. On the right side of the screen you have 2 options to invite people:
- People that are already on Alkemio. You can find them by typing there name. Select the correct user and send the invite.
- People that do not yet have an account on Alkemio. You can invite them by typing their e-mailadress.
